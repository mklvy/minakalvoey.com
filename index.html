The Waiting Room

























The Waiting Room [2022] is a non-linear story and game about inventing your own reality, and not just consuming it. In the Waiting Room you play as a character who got lost in a desperate search for entertainment, and uncover their story through a multiple-choice text interface.





Grand Opening!





























Grand Opening! [2021] is a LARP based on research done on BER Airport. It consists of 11 characters, a backstory, campaigns and rules.
