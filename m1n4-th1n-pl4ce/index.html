restart

The light disappeared for 24 hours and when it returned had put us in between, in the middle.
In daylight it became clear that a huge crater had appeared while it was dark.
The trickster told us to leave behind anything that tied us to the past, throw it in the crater.
Time to look ahead!
The trickster is not a real ruler, nor is the crater a real place.
You are not to seek it out.
Instead, look for the places that are invisible on the maps.
The ones with a real way in and a real way out, places of real transformation, where all limits are relaxed.
Watch out for the trickster.
