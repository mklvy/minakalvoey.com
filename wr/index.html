The Waiting Room
The Waiting Room is a non-linear story and game about inventing your own realities, and not just consuming them.

In The Waiting Room you play as a character who got lost in a desperate search for entertainment, and uncover their story through a multiple-choice text interface.

Click to progress through spaces, but beware. Once you leave a space, you may not be able to return to the same one ever again.

Please wear headphones to play The Waiting Room, and watch as your reality blooms.
Mina Amiri Kalvøy, Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin, 2022 Supervised by Prof. Barbara Junge, Web Development by David Barriga Ruano
